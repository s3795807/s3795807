Melanie Madden

Born in 1983 in Ferntree Gully, Victoria, Australia to 3rd generation Australian immigrants from Ireland and the UK. Spent childhood years in Upwey, at the foot-hills of the Dandenong ranges. Attended schools in various outer-eastern suburbs.

My mum is a teacher, and I have generally considered myself allergic to schooling because of this. I left high school at 16, but returned to the system at 22 to complete my VCE at TAFE. I am stepping way outside of my comfort zone in signing up for this course at the ripe age of 35, but have recently come to the conclusion that I enjoy holidays way too much to be retiring at 65. I have been working in IT for over 10 years and I need to raise the ceiling on my career path – I want more money!

I’m trying my darndest to also enjoy life on the way to retirement (in Mexico, I hope) - I have 2 doggos, a fiancé, I watch reality TV shows like they are sporting competitions (I enjoy all sports), and I’m currently in the process of trying to smart-home my home. Did you know that you can purchase google-home-compatible attachments for roller blinds that turn them into smart blinds?? I just LOVE technology!
