Personal Profile

Test Results

  • Myers-Briggs
  • Learning Style
  • Big-5

There is a few results in these tests that stand out: high scores in assertiveness, prospecting, and agreeableness, and a very low score in aural learning.
The assertiveness score is juxtaposed against turbulence, which is a proposition that I don’t necessarily agree with. I agree with the low score on turbulence: I consider myself calm and level headed. But I don’t really think that that equals assertiveness, and I believe that this opinion is backed up by the high score in agreeableness.
I think that the above behaviors are highly dependent on the environment and people around me. If I am feeling comfortable and happy, then I probably can be assertive. But If I am not, then I have a tendency to shrink in to the background. I need to be aware of this when working in a team if I want my opinion to be heard.
The Myers-Briggs test result gave an overall “type” of mediator. Considering this, I believe I would benefit from being in a team that includes people with very strong opinions, and that those people would benefit from having me in the team. I think that I will be able to bring strong characters and opinions out of people with my agreeable nature, and as a mediator be able to bring these opinions together to form the final product of the teamwork exercise.
